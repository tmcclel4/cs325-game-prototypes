The Black Market

  • High Concept: Buy organs to sell at the highest price!
  • Theme: The theme of this game is organ donor.
  • Mandated Variety: The mandated variety elements for this game are clickable buttons, random number generation, achiever, and the genre is puzzle.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should experience strategic fast-paced clicking to get a high score.
  • Gameplay: The player may click on a button to increase the rate at which their money increases. The payout increase is determined by the counter below the button and players are limited in how many times they can click in a row by an inner timer.
  • Prototype Goal: The main mechanic being tested in this game is the timing of the payout amounts based on math functions.
  • Features: The unique feature of this game is that it uses sine functions to determine the payout amounts instead of a steady counter.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a black market organ dealer trying to make as much money as possible!
  • Story: The Black Market organ trade is booming! As a dealer it is your job to capitalize on this opportunity!
  • Target Audience: The target audience for this game is teens to adults that like timing-intensive games.
  • Play Time: This game takes 30 seconds to play.
  • Strategies: The most effective strategy for this game is timing the button clicks to obtain the highest payout avaiable for that button. Generally the "Heart" button is the most lucrative.
